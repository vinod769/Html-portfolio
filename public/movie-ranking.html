The Best Movies According to Vinod

My top 4 movies of all-time.

July

July is a Telugu action-comedy where a witty and carefree man thwarts a massive bank robbery, setting off a clever and thrilling battle against a dangerous criminal..

Bhahubali

grand Telugu epic where Mahendra Baahubali learns the truth about his father's betrayal and leads a heroic uprising to reclaim his rightful throne and fulfill a legacy of honor and justice.

Pushpa

A fearless laborer rises through the ranks of a red sandalwood smuggling empire, challenging powerful enemies with his raw strength, street smarts, and unbreakable attitude

Pushpa 2

Pushpa returns stronger than ever to defend his empire, face political conspiracies, and assert his rule with vengeance, power, and the iconic swagger that made him unstoppable.
